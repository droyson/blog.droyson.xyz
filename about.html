Well there are 2 main reasons to this.

  1. It's been a little over 6 years that I've been working as a software developer, more precisely a frontend engineer. In all this while I've not had my own website or a tech blog. It's not that common for every frontend dev to have a blog of their own but when you have the skill why not show it off a bit, right?
  2. It turns out that human brain has a limited memory and remembering stuff is a difficult job. So this blog will be a journal to everything that I think is important to remember. Hopefully me documenting stuff might come in handy for someone someday
